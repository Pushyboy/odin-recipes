The Best Lasanga You've Ever Eaten!

Made of layers of freshly cooked pasta, savory meat sauce, and creamy ricotta cheese, this classic Italian dish will take your taste buds on a flavor packed journey. Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large red bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

To assemble the lasagna

  • 1/2 lbs dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 lbs mozarrella cheese
  • 1/4 lb frshly grated Parmesan

Method

  1. Put the Pasta water on to a boil on high heat
  2. Brown the ground beef on medium-high until lightly brown
  3. Take the beef out and put the vegetables and spices in
  4. Sit and cook the vegetables on low for 5 minutes
  5. Add spices to the sauce along with tomato sauce and wine
  6. Bring the sauce to a low simmer for 10-45 minutes and then remove
  7. Boil and drain the Lasagna
  8. Assemble the Lasagna and bake at 375 for 45 minutes
  9. Cool, enjoy, and serve!
